\subsection{Coherence-Preserving Noise Response}
\label{sec:fix05_noise_slope}

We separate generic retrieval noise from coherence fragmentation by
comparing random off-topic replacement with same-topic, answer-absent
replacement from the query's top-20 pool. The latter preserves topical
coherence better while removing direct answer support. We compare both
slopes against HCPC-v1 refinement.
