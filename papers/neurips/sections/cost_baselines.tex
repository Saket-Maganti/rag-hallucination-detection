\section{Cost-Aware Baseline Audit}
\label{sec:cost_baselines}

% POLISH FIX 4: Moved Self-RAG out of the main matched-harness table/figure
% and into the appendix because its backbone, quantization, fine-tuning, and
% reflection-oriented outputs make it a harness-mismatched comparison.
We compare HCPC-v2, CRAG \citep{yan2024crag}, RAPTOR-2L
\citep{raptor2024} in a matched short-answer harness. Self-RAG
\citep{asai2024selfrag} is reported separately in the supplement: its
released fine-tuned Llama-2-7B
checkpoint, 8-bit inference path, reflective decoding behavior, and
entailment-style scoring make that comparison harness-conditional.

\end{table}

HCPC-v2 is competitive on SQuAD, where RAPTOR-2L is slightly higher in
faithfulness and lower in p99 latency. On HotpotQA, CRAG has higher
faithfulness, lower hallucination, and lower latency. Self-RAG numbers are in
the supplement; a fair evaluation requires a scoring protocol that accounts
for reflective decoding rather than treating every extra token as ordinary
answer content.
