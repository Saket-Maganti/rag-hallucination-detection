Better local retrieval, higher context coherence, a single faithfulness
metric, an in-domain threshold, or one favorable baseline row does not
identify RAG answer support. The central durable contribution is the
fixed-generation metric-fragility finding---the same outputs imply materially
different intervention magnitudes under different scorers, and human
calibration shows none can be treated as unqualified ground truth. We release
\method{} as the minimum standard of evidence for making RAG faithfulness
claims; a paper that omits paired contrasts, multiple scorers, human
calibration on disagreements, threshold transfer, p99 latency, or indexing
cost is, by this standard, under-identified.
