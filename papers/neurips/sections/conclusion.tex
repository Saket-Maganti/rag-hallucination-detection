\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

This paper turns a weakened refinement-paradox story into a stronger audit
result: better local retrieval quality, higher context coherence, a single
faithfulness metric, an in-domain threshold, or one favorable baseline row
does not identify RAG answer support. The central durable contribution is
the fixed-generation metric-fragility finding---the same outputs imply
materially different intervention magnitudes under different scorers, and
human calibration shows none can be treated as unqualified ground truth.
We release \method{} so that scale checks, multi-metric and
human-calibrated scoring, transfer tests, and cost accounting become the
standard against which RAG faithfulness claims are evaluated.
