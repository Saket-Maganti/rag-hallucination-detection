\paragraph{The collapse is informative.}
The audit weakens the simple refinement-paradox story: the pilot effect becomes
small under DeBERTa, the matched CCS test rejects the strong coherence
interpretation, and the baseline ranking changes with dataset and cost surface.
That is the point. Standard RAG reporting under-identifies faithfulness claims.

\paragraph{What can and cannot be claimed.}
Local retrieval quality and answer faithfulness can decouple; CCS is diagnostic,
not causal; metric choice materially changes intervention magnitude; human
calibration gives only moderate alignment for the closest automatic scorer;
threshold transfer is uneven; and baseline comparisons require hallucination,
p99 latency, and indexing cost. The audit does not show that HCPC-v2 solves
hallucination, that the DeBERTa paradox is large and stable at scale, that
Self-RAG is globally weak, or that any automatic scorer is a universal
faithfulness metric. Future claims that omit an axis of
Section~\ref{sec:controlledrag_standard} should be marked under-identified.
