\subsection{Self-Confidence Partial Correlations}
\label{sec:fix09_partial_confidence}

We test whether CCS predicts model self-confidence after controlling for
mean query-passage similarity and passage redundancy. If the partial
correlation does not remain positive and significant, the confidence result
is reported as suggestive rather than confirmatory.
